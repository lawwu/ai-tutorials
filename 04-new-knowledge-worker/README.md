## Overview

A talk on what it means to work effectively in 2026 when AI agents can write code, run research, manage memory, and coordinate with tools on your behalf. Covers the Agentic AI Stack mental model and practical strategies for becoming a more effective knowledge worker.

**Topics:**
- Progression of AI: 2022 to 2026
- The Agentic AI Stack (Model → Harness → Memory → Skills/Connectors)
- Harness engineering mindset
- Memory: CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md, Obsidian, personal brain
- Skills, connectors, and features (hooks, subagents, computer use)
- Live demos: agentic ML, autoresearch, Codex chief of staff
- The new knowledge worker mindset (80/20 rule)
- Downsides and pitfalls of AI
